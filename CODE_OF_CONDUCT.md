# HtmlForge Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment‑free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful, constructive, and professional.
- Be open to feedback and focus on community benefit.
- Show empathy toward other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Sexualized language or imagery; unwelcome advances.
- Trolling, insults, and personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others’ private information without consent.

## Scope
- Applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels, as well as public spaces when representing the project.

## Reporting
- Report incidents or concerns to: k.yagci@students.htl-leonding.ac.at
- We will review reports promptly and maintain confidentiality to the extent possible.

## Enforcement
- Maintainers may take any appropriate action, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.

## Enforcement Guidelines
- Correction: private feedback for minor, first-time incidents.
- Warning: official warning for single serious incident or repeated behavior.
- Temporary Ban: time-limited removal for continued misconduct.
- Permanent Ban: long-term removal for persistent or severe misconduct.
